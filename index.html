🪙 Negotiation Chips ▼
🌊 AZURE Trade Tax
Nations that trade without involving Azure owe 30% of every resource exchanged in that deal — paid to Azure. Involving Azure in a trade exempts both parties from the tax.
⚡ SYNAPSE Tech Tax
Charges 1 Capital per minute for any nation using online research during the simulation. The fee applies only while actively researching online — not during negotiations.
🏭 IRONVALE Free Materials
Receives free building materials every round. Ironvale can offer Raw Materials as a bargaining chip without spending their own reserves.
🌿 VERDANTIA Green Cert
Any nation allied with Verdantia gains −3 Pollution per round (Green Certification). Only Verdantia can grant this benefit — and only while the alliance holds.
☀️ NOVAN Land Trade
Controls the most open territory in the region. Novan can trade or lease land to other nations as a unique bargaining chip unavailable to anyone else.
